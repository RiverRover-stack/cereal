## 4. Benefit / Feature Sections

### A forecast you can actually reason about
Every prediction comes with a short "why" panel — plain-English drivers like a strong recent sales trend or a recurring weekday spike. You're not asked to trust a black box; you're shown what's pulling the number up or down.

### No store connection, no signup
Some tools only work if you hand over live access to your store. Trends Arc runs on a CSV export you already have — no OAuth, no app install, and no permissions review before you see a result.

### Built for stores with real, imperfect history
It works with the sales history you actually have — including shorter or gappy records. If there isn't enough data for a dependable forecast, we tell you plainly instead of returning a shaky number.

### Free to try, results in seconds
Upload, get a 30-day revenue forecast with its explanation, and decide on reordering or ad spend the same minute. No trial timer, no locked features on the core forecast.

## 5. FAQ

**Do I need to connect my Shopify store?**
No. Trends Arc runs on a CSV export of your order history — date, revenue, and units sold. There's no OAuth connection, app install, or store-permission step.

**Is this free?**
Yes. Uploading your history and getting a 30-day forecast with its explanation is free, and it doesn't require an account or a credit card.

**How accurate is the forecast?**
Accuracy depends on the quality and length of your own sales history — a store with a longer, cleaner record gets a more dependable forecast than one with a few sparse weeks. Rather than ask you to take the number on faith, we show the factors behind it so you can judge whether it matches what you know is happening in your business. If your history is too thin to forecast reliably, we say so instead of guessing.

**Is my data stored?**
Your forecast is generated from the file you upload for that session. We don't require an account, and there's no saved forecast history in this version. (See our Privacy Policy for the specifics.)

**What do I need in the CSV?**
Columns for date, revenue, and units sold. A SKU column is fine to include but isn't required — the current forecast is store-level, not per-product.
